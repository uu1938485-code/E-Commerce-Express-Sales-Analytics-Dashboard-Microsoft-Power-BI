# E-Commerce-Express-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Microsoft-Power-BI
![image alt]( https://github.com/uu1938485-code/E-Commerce-Express-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Microsoft-Power-BI/blob/f3196a936185db1feb7427db751002b76d575bb8/Dashboard%204_page-0001.jpg)

An interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze revenue performance, product trends, category insights, and state-wise sales distribution across the Indian market.

**Key Dashboard Highlights**

**KPI Overview**
  
Total Revenue: $1.25B | Avg Order Value: $112.85K | Orders: 15.69K | Cancellation Rate: 29.72%

**Revenue by Category**
  
Laptops lead with highest revenue, followed by Mobile and Headphones

**Top Products Performance**
  
MacBook is the top revenue-generating product, followed by OnePlus and Sony

**State-wise Revenue Analysis**
  
Maharashtra is the highest contributing state

**Monthly Revenue Trend**
  
Consistent performance with a peak in July and a drop in November

**Category Filter (Slicer)**
  
Interactive filtering across 9 product categories

**Key Insights**
  
Total revenue reached $1.25 Billion

Laptops dominate overall sales performance

High cancellation rate (29.72%) indicates improvement area

Maharashtra leads in regional sales

Revenue remained stable throughout most of the year
